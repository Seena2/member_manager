Membership Type

According to Article 6 of EVA by-law, the association has the following types of membership

1.Regular Members

Including founding members and members admitted by the general assembly

A locally registered veterinarian who has completed education

Accepting the association's objectives and goals

Aged above 20 years

Complies with the article of association and general assembly decisions

Able to pay contribution fees decided by the general assembly

Not legally prohibited

Annual membership fee is 300ETB, members registering for first time will be required to pay 350ETB

2.Cooperative Member

An apprentice and a veterinarian candidate

Professionals who are operating the veterinary and related activities such activities approved by the management board to meet the objectivies of the association.

Supporting organizations can be cooperative member

Annual membership fee: 2000 Birr

3.Life time Member

Any regular member of the association can be a life time member by advance payment of twenty years membership contribution fees.

A life time member is not expected to pay the annual contribution fees and can exercise in/her life time the entitlements of the membership.

4.Honorary Member

Those who contributed to the success of the association without being member and those who are engaged with the association becoming role models in succeeding in good performances and become members in accordance with the decisions of the General Assembly.

The honorary members have NO right to vote and to be contenders.

Unless with self-motion, they are not obligated to pay for contribution and other fees.
